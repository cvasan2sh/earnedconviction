Most of my work has involved designing and scaling revenue systems across enterprise technology and SaaS. Lately I've been experimenting with small AI-driven tools built using agentic workflows.

My career has sat at the intersection of markets, enterprise technology, and revenue execution.

## Career

I studied Engineering Physics at IIT Madras and later Management at IIM Calcutta.

I started my career trading derivatives in global FX markets, primarily EUR/USD futures along with other major currency pairs and gold. Trading builds a particular mindset: decisions under uncertainty, probabilistic thinking, and fast feedback loops.

From there I moved into enterprise consulting at Wipro, working with CIO organizations on technology portfolio rationalization and strategy. The work focused on simplifying complex enterprise architectures, reducing total cost of ownership, and redirecting capital into modernization initiatives such as cloud migration, analytics platforms, and automation.

Later I managed a $13M data services portfolio for a global banking client involving roughly 250 engineers across multiple delivery programs. The portfolio I inherited was declining; stabilizing delivery and launching new initiatives turned it into a growing business.

After that I moved into the SaaS ecosystem.

At Hevo Data, I worked on scaling the commercial revenue engine for a data infrastructure platform, leading new business sales across SMB and mid-market segments while building and managing teams across AEs, SDRs, and BDRs.

Today at Freshworks, I lead North America outbound new business for the SMB and Commercial segments. My focus is building scalable pipeline systems: structuring outbound teams, improving AE productivity, and aligning marketing, partnerships, and sales execution to drive predictable growth.

Across these roles I've sold and worked across several layers of enterprise technology: IT services, cloud migration, data infrastructure, analytics platforms, and AI-driven software.
